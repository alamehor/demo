Polish lemmetizer demo

Lemmatization is the process of grouping together forms of a word so they can be analysed as a single item, identified by the word's lemma, or dictionary form. - Wikipedia

To present how lemmatization can be used in real world situations, we have implemented a simple search tool that looks for words in an invocation to "Pan Tadeusz" - a classic polish work of art written by Adam Mickiewicz. The search tool tries to match words provided in a form in two ways:
by comparing words directly and by comparing words' lemmas
Because polish language is very complex, searching direct matches of words would often return nothing. Instead, we can return words that have same lemmas as searched word, which yields more useful results.
