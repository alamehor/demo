Polish lemmetizer demo

Lemmatisation (or lemmatization) in linguistics is the process of grouping together the inflected forms of a word so they can be analysed as a single item, identified by the word's lemma, or dictionary form. - Wikipedia

To present how lemmatization can be used in real world situations, we have implemented a simple search tool that looks for words in a classic polish work of art - "Pan Tadeusz" by Adam Mickiewicz. The search tool tries to match words provided in a form in two ways:

  • by comparing words directly
  • by comparing words' lemmas, or dictionary forms
Because polish language is very complex, searching direct matches of words would often return nothing, lemmatization partialy solves this problems, allowing for a much more meaningful search.

Type a sentence or list of words to search for their appearences.
