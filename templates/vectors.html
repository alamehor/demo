Word similarity demo

Vectorization is the process of embedding words (tokens) into vectors of floating point numbers. Good vectorizaition allows us to calculate word similarity simply by using euclidean distance between the vectors.

Generating word vectors and adding them to spaCy not only allows us to calculate similarities between various polish words, but is also an important step on the way to training neural networks - these vectors will serve as their input.
