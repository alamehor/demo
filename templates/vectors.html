Word similarity demo

Representing words as vectors for easy analysis and machine learning.

Vectorization is the process of embedding words (tokens) into vectors of floating point numbers. Good vectorizaition allows us to calculate word similarity simply by measuring euclidean distance between the vectors. Generating word vectors and adding them to spaCy not only allows us to calculate similarities between various polish words, but is also an important step on the way to training neural networks - these vectors will serve as their input.

As a proof of concept, we have trained our word vectors using word2vec on NKJP corpus, a proven technique on the most popular polish dataset. We also plan to train a set of models using Facebook's fasttext set of vectors, which is widely regarded as a state-of-the-art solution.
